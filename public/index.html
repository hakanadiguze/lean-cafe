How it works

A complete Lean Café retrospective in 5 phases

1
✍️ Write Cards
Everyone writes cards about what they want to discuss — one topic per card. All cards are visible to the whole group. The facilitator sets a timer. When the timer ends, the facilitator opens voting.
2
🗳️ Vote
Everyone gets a fixed number of votes. Vote for the cards you most want to discuss. You can spread votes or stack them. When voting closes, the top cards become discussion rooms.
3
🚪 Choose a Room
Each top-voted card becomes a room. Participants choose which room to join. The facilitator creates matching breakout rooms in Microsoft Teams — join the same-numbered room in Teams too.
4
💡 Discuss
Groups discuss their topic in the Teams breakout room. One person (the Scribe) enters the root cause and action items. Each action needs a description, an assignee (max 20 chars), and a due date.
5
📄 Report
When all rooms are done, the facilitator ends the session. A full PDF report is generated with all cards, root causes, and action items.
